# User Guide (Web Interface)

## Getting Started

1. **Registration**: Click "Регистрация" (Register/Sign up) on the home page. Fill in username, email, and password. You will be automatically logged in.
2. **Login**: If you already have an account, click "Войти" (Login) and enter your credentials.

## Managing Profile

1. **View Profile**: Click on your username or avatar in the top navigation bar. You will see your public profile information.
2. **Edit Profile**:
   - On the Profile View page, click the "Редактировать" (Edit) button.
   - You can update your **First Name**, **Last Name**, **Email**, **Bio**, and **Avatar**.
   - Click "Сохранить" (Save) to apply changes.
   - Use "Отмена" (Cancel) to return to the view mode without saving.

## Photo Albums (Dashboard)

- **Dashboard**: Accessed via "Мои Альбомы" (My Albums). Shows a list of your created albums sorted by date.
- **Create Album**:
  - Click "Create Album" (or use provided Create Album view).
  - Provide a Title and Description.
  - **Upload Photos**:
    - Click to select files or **Drag & Drop** photos into the upload area.
    - View previews of selected photos.
    - Remove individual photos before uploading by clicking the 'X' on the preview.
    - You can add more batches of photos before creating the album.
- **Viewing an Album**:
  - Browse photos in a grid layout.
  - **Collage**: Click the "Скачать коллаж (PNG)" button to automatically generate and download a collage of all photos in the album.
  - **Visibility**: Toggle album visibility (Public/Private) using the toggle button.
  - **Share**: If public, copy the share link to send to others.

## Features

- **Cloudinary Storage**: Secure and reliable storage for avatars and album photos (in production mode).
- **Clean UI**: The interface uses a clean, CSS-based design without heavy frontend frameworks, ensuring fast load times.
